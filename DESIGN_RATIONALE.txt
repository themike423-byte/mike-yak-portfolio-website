THE SOLUTION: PROGRESSIVE DISCLOSURE
------------------------------------

**Design Principle:** Show the most detailed information first (technology partners with their 20 subcategories), then collapse higher-level partner types (GSIs, consulting, channel) into expandable sections.

**Why This Works:**

1. **Cognitive Load Management**
   - Technology partners are your deepest expertise â†’ Open by default
   - Users can digest flip cards for tech partners immediately
   - GSI/Consulting/Channel are simpler â†’ Collapsed until needed
   - Reduces initial visual complexity from 430+ items to ~100 visible cards

2. **Information Hierarchy**
   - Most technical detail = Most prominent placement
   - Strategic partnerships (GSIs) = Secondary tier
   - Tactical partnerships (channel) = Tertiary tier
   - Mirrors how partnerships actually work in enterprise sales

3. **Visual Consistency**
   - Technology: Maintains existing flip card interaction (hover to reveal)
   - GSIs/Consulting/Channel: Simple logo grid (click header to expand)
   - Different interaction patterns for different content density
   - All use same color palette, shadows, and spacing system
